We're hiring!

Hello everyone! We’re happy to announce that we are recruiting second-year students for the 2021/22 school year! You will get the chance to work behind the scenes and see where all the magic happens!

Through this experience, you will get to help several high school students entering post-secondary while building your leadership and communication skills and expanding your knowledge. You will also get the chance to expand your network and add experience to your resume.

The application process is easy! All you need to do is click the link to sign up, select the role you want to apply for, answer the questions on the form, and interview with us! We look forward to getting to know you all! Hope to see you soon, and spread the word!

Marketing (Social Media Coordinators) - 3 position(s)

Requirements

  • 2nd year student attending a post-secondary institution within Canada.
  • Experience with content creation softwares (i.e. canva*, adobe suite).
  • Social media savvy (Especially with Instagram and TikTok).
  • Communicate professionally over emails/video calls.
  • Keen attention to details and willingness to learn.
  • Previous marketing-related experience is an asset but not required.
  • Submission of any previous art/design/photography work will be required.

Job Description

  • Brainstorming and contributing ideas for bridges content (e.g. social media posts and newsletters).
  • Formatting and designing the official bridges newsletter.
  • Overseeing and ensuring bridges social media accounts fit brand aesthetic.
  • Responsible for creating and posting unique social media and promotional content that aligns with business goals. and increases brand awareness.
  • Utilize social media analytics in order to improve audience interaction.
  • Attending biweekly meetings with the marketing team to recap, create, and strategize.
  • Work is remote and at your own pace but ~ 2 hours of commitment/week.

Development (Junior Front/Back End Developers) - 2 positon(s)

Requirements

  • General requirements:
    • 2nd year student attending a post-secondary institution within Canada.
    • Ability to communicate and coordinate with other team members, through emails or video calls.
    • Contribute and brainstorm creative ideas to improve the overall service.
  • Front-end requirements:
    • Knowledge of basic front-end practices (should know HTML, CSS and Javascript).
    • Ability to quickly learn and adapt to new softwares and programs.
    • Submission of a project or website created in the past to demonstrate knowledge in front-end is optional, but highly recommended.
  • Back-end requirements:
    • Knowledge of basic back-end practices and some experience working with back-end frameworks or databases.
    • Experience working with Django would be an asset but is not required.
    • A project with some back-end functionality is preferred, but highly recommended.

Job Description

  • General requirements:
    • Brainstorming and contributing ideas for bridges newsletters.
    • Maintenance of the current bridges website and future modifications.
    • Work in coordination with Head Developers to integrate new features (new pages, sections or logic) into the bridges website.
    • Work on email utilities, form creation, google analytics, and podcast editing (training will be provided but experience is an asset).
    • Google analytics and SEO improvements will be made by both our front-end and back-end developers.
    • All dev work will be done remotely but will be approximately 2-5 hrs/week commitment (depending on the projects in progress).
  • Front-end requirements:
    • Front-end developers will focus on taking care of UI issues or updates to our site as well as sending out our biweekly newsletters.
    • Create forms for applications, surveys, etc and link them to our site.
    • Submission of a project or website created in the past to demonstrate knowledge in front-end is optional, but highly recommended.
  • Back-end requirements:
    • Back-end developers will focus on taking care of complicated bugs, issues or updates.
    • Work alongside the Head of Back-end development to manage the database and create any needed additions.
