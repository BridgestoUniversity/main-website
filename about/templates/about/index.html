Who are we?

Bridges to University is a free student support initiative designed to help ease the transition from high school to post-secondary. We aim to inspire students as they take their first step into their careers by providing a central location for advice or answers to any questions related to post-secondary education, student life and much more!

Get involved!

Our Vision

At bridges, we aim to help build the next generation of students into strong and confident individuals and lead them into their future. We plan on doing this by providing them with the support they need to make the right academic and career choices for them.

Our Values

At bridges, we hold many key values close to our hearts. These include positivity, genuinity, the pursuit of knowledge, honesty and integrity, as well as accountability and trustworthiness. Most of all however, we value the quality of our content and your satisfaction with our work!

Our Team

Our team at bridges consists of nine individuals who attend five different universities and are enrolled in nine different programs, located all over the province.
